Fusion

Fusion Restaurant offers an eclectic mix of International Cuisines, set within an ambient, relaxed and sophisticated environment. Fusion gives you elegant dining at its best.

Our team of Internationally trained Chefs create mouth-watering dishes from around the world, using the finest of local and imported produce. Our wide selection of fine wines compliment our dishes and our selection of Home Made Desserts will complete your meal. From a romantic dinner for two, family celebration or executive business dinner, Fusion will provide the perfect environment with the finest cuisine.
